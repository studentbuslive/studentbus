About Student Bus

Student Bus is a mobile application designed to help parents and students track their school and college bus in real-time.

Our app provides peace of mind by offering live GPS tracking, instant notifications, and route information. Parents can know exactly where their child's bus is and when it will arrive.

Track your child's school bus anytime, anywhere with Student Bus!

Key Features

Live GPS Tracking

Track your child's school bus location in real-time on a map. See exactly where the bus is at any moment.

Real-time Notifications

Get instant alerts when the bus is approaching your stop. Never miss the bus again.

ETA Updates

Know exactly when the bus will arrive at your pickup point. Plan your morning better.

Route Information

View complete route details with all stops and timings. Know the entire journey.

Driver Contact

Easy one-tap calling to the bus driver when needed. Stay connected always.

Safe and Secure

Bank-grade security to protect your family's data. Your privacy is our priority.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Student Bus is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number

Child Information: Child's name, school name, bus route, pickup point

Location Data: Bus GPS location (only during active trips for tracking)

Payment Information: Processed securely by Razorpay. We do not store card details.

How We Use Your Information

To provide real-time bus tracking, send notifications, process payments, and improve services.

Data Security

All data is encrypted using industry-standard protocols. We use secure servers and restrict access to authorized personnel only.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with payment processors (Razorpay) and as required by law.

Your Rights

You can access, correct, or delete your data. Contact us at studentbuslive@gmail.com for any privacy concerns.
